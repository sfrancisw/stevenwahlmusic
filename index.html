About

CV

Who are you?

Hey there I'm Steven!
I'm a 21 year old guy from Germany who loves making music.

What do you enjoy besides music?

I love programming and anything tech related really. I'm actually studying IT right now!
I also enjoy playing some games, watching movies or reading a good book, anything that gets my creative gears up and running. I'm also a huge D&D fan!
Oh and chess. I love chess.

What are your favorite games?

There are a lot of games I really like. My top three are Outer Wilds, Hollow Knight and Life is Strange.
But all the Pokémon games (especially the Mystery Dungeon series) have a special place in my heart.

When did you start making music?

I've been doing music my whole life. My first instrument that I played was the xylophone followed by the recorder during kindergarten. I picked up the trumpet when I was 8 years old and it has been my main instrument since then. I also have a guitar, keyboard, kalimba and harmonica which I occasionally play.
I've been composing since 2018.

Why did you become a composer?

I still remember the day I knew I wanted to become a composer. It was Halloween 2018 and I was in my room watching youtube videos. Then I saw a behind the scenes video for the music of ARK: Survival Evolved with Gareth Coker. The video ignited a flame inside of me that will never extinguish. When I saw all these musicians in a recording room playing music that he made himself I just thought "Wow that is so awesome and cool, I want to do that one day too".
I then downloaded Musescore and spent the day creating my first melody.
Now I'm trying to become better and better at what I'm doing so that one day I'll be able to stand where he stood and be able to feel this amazing experience.

What are your rates?

Every project is different so unfortunately I can't tell you a fixed number. If you want to hire me or even if you're just considering it, do not hesitate to contact me! This way I get to see your awesome project and we can discuss pricing.

How can I be certain that you're the right composer for my project?

You can listen to my portfolio and answer that question for yourself. You can also request a COMPLETELY FREE demo track for your project that is about 1 minute long. After I send you the demo you can choose whether you want to work with me or look for another composer. Either way, feel free to ask me for a demo if you're interested!

How do I hire you?

Feel free to contact me. You can tell me about your project and your ideas for the music. If you have any screenshots or video clips of your project I'd love to see them as well! Then we can discuss things like expectations, deadlines, music style, pricing, etc. That's pretty much it. :D
